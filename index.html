How to play Who Would Win

Who Would Win is a debate party game with 1v1 rounds that take 2 minutes to play.

Place this phone in the center of the table, so everyone can see the matchup. Keep another phone handy to run a timer. You don't need any other phones or materials.

Pick two players to debate, who each embody a different character. They must make their case for why their character would win the round's matchup.

Character 1 has 30 seconds to speak uninterrupted. Character 2 then has 30 seconds to respond uninterrupted. Finally, Character 1 has 10 seconds to make a closing argument.

All other players vote on the winner, then pick a new matchup with new players. You can keep track of wins if you'd like, but the fun is in making the case for outrageous scenarios!
